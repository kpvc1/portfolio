Myanmar Manuscript Digital Library (MMDL) - University of Toronto

The Myanmar Manuscript Digital LIbrary is a growing collection of open-access Pali and Pali-Burmese manuscripts available to scholars all over the world, curated by the University of Toronto.

Launched in 2019, I contributed to the initial design and development of the website. In collaboration with UofT Systems Administrators, I designed and developed the website using the Genesis framework in Wordpress. I was also tasked to translate, sort and implement digital manuscripts into the database.

The live website can be viewed at https://mmdl.utoronto.ca/
