Branding a Toronto Jazz Café

Decadance aims to carve out a unique space that invites patrons to immerse themselves in the joy of live jazz and culinary excellence. Every element was curated to establish a cohesive identity aligned with the mission of providing an exceptional dining experience in the city of Toronto.

The selected colors, typography, and decorative motifs evoke sophistication and warmth, reflecting on the history of jazz while appealing to modern sensibilities, making the space an inviting atmosphere that encourages customers to dine, dance and enjoy live performances.

Brainstorming + Research

The research and brainstorming process began by analyzing the café's cultural and aesthetic context. Jazz, rooted in history and creativity, called for a visual identity that would reflect both its vintage charm and timeless appeal.

Inspiration was drawn from the Art Deco movement and post-war modernism. I aimed to combine the sleek geometric elegance of the 1920's Art Deco style and the cultural and artistic Jazz movement of the 1940's to portray a complex and introspective style of abstract expression with minimalist forms.

Brand Planning

Typography

A bold Art Deco styled font was chosen for it's lines and sharp angles, reinforcing a sense of sophistication and modernity.

Color Guide

A colour palette of light tan and pale blue, complemented with shades of black and white, evoke a sense of class, rustic charm and elegance.

Logo Design

Wordmark

A wordmark design featuring the chosen typeface embodies the energy and sophistication of the brand. The strong lines and angular details of the typeface evoke the sleek, geometric aesthetics of Art Deco, while still feeling modern. Small diamond ornaments add a touch of decorative flair while enhancing the luxurious and timeless appeal of the overall design.

Stylized Art-Deco Logo

To better capture the essence of the café, an alternate stylized logo alongside the wordmark was designed. This decision was driven by the desire to create a visual identity that not only reflects the vibrant jazz culture but also resonates with both locals and visitors. To achieve this, I incorporated sharp Art Deco lines and motifs into a Toronto skyline featuring the C.N. Tower.

Signage

The signage for Decadance features a large full-window logo, designed to create a striking visual impact. The choice of a white vinyl allows for excellent visibility against the café's interior, ensuring that the branding is easily recognizable both during the day and at night.

Packaging

A thoughtfully designed range of items, including takeout bowls and boxes, containers, coffee mugs, cups, coasters, and more. Each piece features the café's distinct logo and color palette, creating a cohesive visual identity that enhances the customer experience. These branding elements create a memorable and cohesive impression, reinforcing the café’s commitment to quality and aesthetic appeal.
